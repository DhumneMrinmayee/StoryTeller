“Well, I haven’t.” You told it, “I don’t know what that is.”  
  
It sighed, “I’m going to check this out, whatever it is.”  
  
You felt it start to drift out of you, but, quickly, you cried out, “Wait! I’ll go with you!”  
  
“You’re not sure, though. You’re still not sure, are you?”  
  
“No, I’m not. But I’m willing to listen.”
